About This Lab Environment

This is a controlled, isolated Docker-based cybersecurity training environment designed to simulate Denial of Service (DoS) attack conditions for educational analysis.

Purpose

This lab demonstrates how HTTP flood, connection exhaustion, and high-rate traffic affect web server performance — enabling DFIR analysts, SOC engineers, and security students to study attack signatures, monitoring workflows, and mitigation strategies safely.
